Ginger Cat Coffee

At Bobcats Coffee, we proudly serve Ginger Cat Coffee’s Buddy Blend—a rich, smooth, and perfectly balanced roast. Crafted for true coffee lovers, it delivers a bold yet comforting flavor in every cup.

making every sip a delightful experience. Whether you prefer it black, with milk, or as a specialty brew, this blend is made to be enjoyed.

Alternative Milks

At Bobcats Coffee, we believe that everyone deserves to enjoy their perfect cup of coffee, which is why we offer a variety of alternative milks, including oat, almond, soy, and coconut.

These options aren’t just about preference—they’re about inclusivity, health, and sustainability.

eco environment

At Bobcats Coffee, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. We offer a variety of alternative milks, including oat, almond, soy, and coconut, to provide options for different dietary needs while reducing our environmental impact. By choosing plant-based milks, you're helping us minimize our carbon footprint.

We also ensure our coffee, like the Ginger Cat Coffee Buddy Blend, is ethically sourced, supporting sustainable farming practices.

At Bobcats Coffee, we are more than just a coffee shop – we are a community hub in the heart of Colwyn Bay. Founded by Bobby, a passionate 20-year-old with a love for coffee, we strive to create a space where everyone feels at home.

Whether you’re sipping on a freshly brewed coffee, enjoying a delicious homemade pastry, or spending time with your furry friends, our goal is to provide a welcoming and relaxed atmosphere for all. We believe in quality, sustainability, and the power of a good cup of coffee to bring people together.

We pride ourselves on using only the best ingredients, including our signature Ginger Cat Coffee’s Buddy Blend, to craft every cup to perfection.

We also offer a variety of plant-based milk options, ensuring that everyone can enjoy their favorite beverages just the way they like them. At Bobcats Coffee, our mission is simple: to offer exceptional coffee, create a cozy space for connection, and be a positive force in our local community.

We would like to extend a heartfelt thank you to our incredible community for supporting Bobcats Coffee.

Your continued loyalty, warmth, and positive energy make our coffee shop the special place it is today. We’re so grateful to serve you and your furry companions, and we truly value the connections we’ve made with each and every one of you.

Your support not only helps us grow but also strengthens the sense of community we cherish. Thank you for being a part of the Bobcats Coffee family — we couldn’t do it without you!
